PIG

DICE

The objective of the Pig Dice Game is to score 100 points before your opponent does. The game is played with a six-sided die. A player can continue rolling the die until they are happy with the number of points you scored, after which the player can end the turn and save their score. If a player rolls a one, they lose all the points accumulated during that turn, and lose the turn.
